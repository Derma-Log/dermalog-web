Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Derma.Log is designed for private skin observation records. The app helps users maintain structured notes and image-based history for personal observation over time.

Not a diagnostic service

Derma.Log does not provide medical diagnosis, medical certainty, emergency medical support, or treatment recommendations. If you have a medical concern, contact a qualified healthcare professional.

Your records

Users own and control their records. Derma.Log is intended to support private record keeping and personal organization of skin observations.
